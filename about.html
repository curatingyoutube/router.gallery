About

The router is used as an autonomous gallery space to show digital artworks. The router distributes the artworks through its lokal network. All exhibitions are only accessible at the local site. The router includes a small web-server on which all artworks can be placed and arranged by the curator. The access is granted through an open WLAN (Wireless Local Area Network) over WIFI.
This type of net art show brings on one hand back the intimate character of the Net-Art-Activities to a site specific relation and on the other hand it opens up a possibility for an discursive reception in a public situation. This website is the timetable for the router-gallery-space installed at the panke.club in Berlin-Wedding. The website will announce the exhibition events and will provide infos about the artists and curators. The router.gallery acts here like a independent satellite of the panke.gallery and will be curated in a monthly rhythm by various guest curators.

router.gallery is maintained by curatingyoutube and supported by panke e.V. This page is based on a generated GitHub Pages branch and the tactile theme by Jason Long.
