Context

the exhibition presents a collection of home buttons used in old macintosh browsers, which were used to jump straight ahead to the homepage. Through the decline of the so called homepage and the rise of the so called social-network-profiles it seemed that the space for the home button was wasted. At the same time the so called start-page was understood at best as a unavoidable banner which you could sell as a browser developer. Google or Yahoo where paying to be the default. The decision to cut the home button was made at a time where many web companies and in special apple had a faible for the skeuomorphism so that we have here also a beautiful collection from a special moment in time.

About

This is the timetable for the router gallery space installed at the panke.club in Berlin Wedding. The router.gallery acts here like a satellite of the panke.gallery and will be curated in a monthly rhythm by various guest curators. All artworks are accessible only at the local site for ~ 4 weeks.
This type of net.art show brings the real intimate character back to a site and time specific relation in which the used distribution and viewing devices are mostly a private ones. There are only the artists, the recipients and the curator involved.
